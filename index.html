Core Deliverables

Minimal Artifacts.
Maximum Impact.

6 mandatory deliverables fully integrated in Wrike — the single source of truth.

📋
Project Charter
1-Pager
PurposeAlign stakeholders and formally initiate the project
ContentObjective, scope, success criteria, timeline, key stakeholders
FormatSingle-page summary — linked or embedded in Wrike
ValueEnsures clarity and alignment from day one
💼
Business Case
Lightweight
PurposeJustify the initiative based on value and strategic alignment
ContentProblem statement, expected benefits, rough costs, risks, priority level
FormatShort structured document or form in Wrike
ValueEnables better prioritization and resource allocation
🗓
Project Plan
Wrike-Native ✦
PurposeDefine the execution roadmap for the project
ContentTasks, owners, timelines, dependencies, milestones
FormatFully managed inside Wrike — no external files
ValueDrives execution discipline and full transparency
⚠️
RAID Log
Live Tracking
PurposeProactively manage risks and execution constraints
ContentRisks, Assumptions, Issues, Dependencies with owners and status
FormatContinuously updated log — Wrike custom fields or table
ValueReduces surprises and improves decision-making
📊
Status Report
Auto-generated ✦
PurposeProvide real-time visibility to stakeholders
ContentProgress, key milestones, risks, overall health (RAG status)
FormatAuto-generated from Wrike dashboards
ValueEliminates manual reporting and ensures real-time visibility
✅
Closure & Value Report
Value Realization
PurposeValidate outcomes and capture learnings
ContentDelivered scope, KPI results vs plan, lessons learned
FormatShort structured summary — recorded in Wrike
ValueEnsures accountability and continuous improvement
💡

"Minimalist governance model: fewer documents, fully integrated in Wrike, focused on execution and value." — Wrike is the single source of truth. ✦ = Wrike Native
